Meet the US Construction Family of Companies

Five specialized companies, one integrated standard. Here is how each contributes to our vertically integrated construction model.

Quick Answer

The US Construction family consists of five specialized companies: US Framing (commercial wood and metal framing), US Drywall (drywall installation, acoustical ceilings, and fire-rated assemblies), US Interiors (trim, millwork, doors, and interior finishing), US Exteriors (siding, roofing, EIFS, and waterproofing), and US Development (turnkey construction management and real estate development). Together they deliver framing-through-finish services under one umbrella.

The Family at a Glance

US Construction is not one company trying to do everything. It is five specialist companies that each excel at their trade — and together form a vertically integrated construction powerhouse. Here is a closer look at each member of the family.

US Framing: Where It All Began

Founded in the early 1990s, US Framing is the original member of the family. Specializing in commercial wood and metal framing, US Framing handles multifamily housing, student housing, assisted living facilities, and mixed-use developments ranging from 10,000 to 500,000+ square feet.

Key capabilities: Wood framing, metal stud framing, panelized wall systems, structural sheathing, and trusses.

US Drywall: Walls and Ceilings

US Drywall was the natural second addition to the family, capturing the critical framing-to-drywall handoff. Our drywall crews work hand-in-hand with the framing team, moving onto a floor the day after framing is complete — something that rarely happens with independent subcontractors.

Key capabilities: Drywall installation (Level 1-5 finishes), metal stud framing, acoustical ceiling tile and grid, fire-rated assemblies (1-4 hour UL-listed), specialty textures, and skim coating.

US Interiors: The Finishing Touch

US Interiors handles the detail work that turns a construction project into a finished space. Our crews are craftsmen focused on precision, because the finishing touches are what clients see and what tenants experience every day.

Key capabilities: Trim and base installation, crown molding, door and hardware packages, custom millwork, cabinetry, and specialty interior installations.

US Exteriors: The Building Envelope

US Exteriors protects the building from the outside in. Our exterior crews coordinate with US Framing to ensure the structural substrate is prepared correctly for the envelope system, and with US Drywall to ensure interior work is not compromised by moisture intrusion.

Key capabilities: Commercial siding (fiber cement, vinyl, engineered wood), roofing systems, EIFS and stucco, waterproofing and flashing, weather barriers, and window/door installation.

US Development: The Coordinator

US Development is the newest member of the family, formed to offer turnkey construction management and real estate development services. US Development coordinates all five companies on large-scale projects, providing developers and GCs with a single contract and single point of accountability for the entire integrated scope.

Key capabilities: Turnkey construction management, multi-trade coordination, design-build, value engineering, and real estate development.

How to Engage the Family

General contractors can engage any US Construction company individually for specialized scopes, or bundle multiple companies for integrated delivery. The more trades you bundle, the greater the cost savings and schedule benefits. Contact us at (502) 555-0100 or visit any company's website directly to learn more about their specific capabilities.
