Our Story

A family that started with one framing crew and grew into five specialized companies. Vertically integrated since day one.

Vertically Integrated Since Day One

Built by Family. Scaled by Excellence.

US Construction began in the early 1990s as a single framing crew in Louisville, Kentucky. The founder saw an opportunity that most contractors missed: the handoffs between trades were where projects fell apart. Miscommunication between framers and drywall crews. Scheduling gaps between interior and exterior teams. Finger-pointing when quality suffered.

The solution was simple in concept and ambitious in execution â€” build a family of companies that could handle every phase from framing through finish under one umbrella. Start with what you know (framing), then expand into the adjacent trades where coordination matters most.

Over three decades, that vision became reality. US Framing grew into the region's premier commercial framing contractor. US Drywall was added to eliminate the framing-to-drywall handoff. US Interiors brought finishing in-house. US Exteriors completed the building envelope. And US Development tied it all together with turnkey project management.

Today, US Construction operates five specialized companies across seven states, with over 1,000 projects completed and more than $500 million in installed value. But the founding principle has never changed: when one family handles every phase, the result is better, faster, and more accountable.

Our Journey

Three Decades of Growth

1990s

The Foundation

US Framing is founded in Louisville, KY. Starting with a small crew of experienced carpenters, the company quickly earns a reputation for reliable commercial wood framing on multifamily projects.

2000s

Expanding the Family

US Drywall is launched to capture the natural workflow from framing to drywall. Metal stud framing capability is added. The family concept proves itself on projects where both companies work together, cutting transition time by weeks.

2010s

Full Vertical Integration

US Interiors and US Exteriors are established, completing the framing-through-finish vision. The company expands across Kentucky, Tennessee, Indiana, and into the broader Southeast. Project volume crosses the $250M milestone.

2020s

Turnkey Delivery

US Development is formed to offer turnkey construction management, coordinating all five companies on large-scale projects. The family surpasses 1,000 completed projects and $500M in installed value, serving seven states across the Southeast.
